Essays Start-Up Ideas Books/Podcasts

Reading non-fiction books and listening to podcasts give me an endless source of ideas and inspiration. While I focus primarily on entrepreneurship, start-ups, and venture capital topics, I also explore other areas. I tend to have very specific interests that last anywhere from a few months to a year or more before I focus my learning elsewhere. The best ideas usually come from other disciplines and the best people connect concepts and draw comparisons from distant disciplines to draw new insights. I believe that sharing the list of books and podcasts I've engaged with recently will help shed light onto my model of the world, the connections and comparisons I'm exploring, and provide new areas of exploration for you. Learning is a life-long journey and the best thing anyone can learn during college and in their 20s is the ability and desire to pursue continous learning. The most recent books and podcasts are at the top. If you have any feedback, feel free to email me at jeffreyjlee at post dot harvard dot edu.
