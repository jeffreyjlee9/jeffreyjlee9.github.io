Writing provides an outlet for me to sort through my thoughts and ideas. Although the essay topics skew more towards entrepreneurship and start-ups, I like to explore other things as well. As we get older, our repository of knowledge and experiences grows from all the information we take in through watching, listening, and reading, though older information tends to decay and newer information dictates the forefront of our immediate thoughts and conclusions. These essays help me build a permanent, public record of my thoughts as they develop and as I feel compelled to explore and write them down. Each person has a unique, ever-evolving model of the world - my essays below will give you a small sampling of my model and I hope they provide some interesting, undiscovered insights for you. You'll find the most recent essays at the top. If you have any feedback, feel free to email me at jeffreyjlee at post dot harvard dot edu.
